Philosophy

Our basic philosophy is to combine the disciplines of Architecture and Interior Design in a collaborative environment resulting in a superior product in terms of functionality, aesthetics and cost effectiveness. We have found that in projects done by traditional architectural firms, often times the relationships and quality of the interior spaces is of secondary importance to the study of exterior architectural forms and massing. At Ai Collaborative, we place equal importance on the design of interior spaces and how their relationships affect the overall architectural design. This process of transdisciplinary problem solving utilizing architects, interior designers, engineers and the end user results in a superior design product for our clients.


The principals and project managers of Ai Collaborative maintain "hands on" involvement from project inception through completion thereby assuring the client a single source of contact and simplification of the communication and project delivery process.
